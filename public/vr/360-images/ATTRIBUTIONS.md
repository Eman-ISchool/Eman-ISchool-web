## Attribution Template (for future use)

When replacing placeholders with real images, use this template:

```markdown
### [filename].jpg
- **Status:** ✅ Final
- **Source:** [Source name, e.g., "Wikimedia Commons", "Adobe Stock", "Custom Photography"]
- **Author:** [Photographer/Creator name]
- **License:** [License type, e.g., "CC-BY-SA 4.0", "CC0", "Royalty-Free"]
- **URL:** [Original image URL]
- **Date:** [Date accessed or created]
- **Modifications:** [List any modifications: cropping, color correction, etc.]
- **Notes:** [Any additional information]
```

### License Types Reference

- **CC0 (Public Domain):** No restrictions, attribution not required (but appreciated)
- **CC-BY:** Attribution required, free to use and modify
- **CC-BY-SA:** Attribution required, share-alike (derivatives must use same license)
- **CC-BY-NC:** Attribution required, non-commercial use only
- **CC-BY-NC-SA:** Attribution required, non-commercial, share-alike
- **Royalty-Free:** Purchased license, check terms for attribution requirements
- **Custom/Commissioned:** Full rights owned, no attribution needed

## Compliance

All images used in this project must comply with:

1. **Licensing requirements** - Proper licenses for educational/commercial use
2. **Attribution requirements** - Credit given as specified by license
3. **Usage restrictions** - Respect any limitations (non-commercial, no derivatives, etc.)
4. **Cultural sensitivity** - Respectful representation of historical and cultural sites
5. **Photography permissions** - Legal rights to photograph at locations
